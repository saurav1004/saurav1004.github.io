Writing for Engineers, by Engineers

Engineering is a Performance Art. Quite Literaly.

You would find here a curated list of blogs that I love reading. This is in no way a complete list. The list is made into two parts : Systems and Machine Learning.

Systems #

Databases, Distributed Systems, Programming Languages, Compilers, Operating Systems etc.

Brendan Gregg – Deep dives into systems performance and observability.

Justine Tunney – Unique perspectives on low-level programming and software history.

Dan Luu – Insightful essays on engineering culture and technical tradeoffs.

Andreas Kling – Chronicles the development of SerenityOS and software craftsmanship.

Eli Bendersky – Clear explanations of programming concepts and language internals.

Hillel Wayne – Thoughtful writing on formal methods and software engineering practices.

Chris Siebenmann – Practical explorations of Unix systems and networking.

Murat Demirbas – Accessible research summaries on distributed systems.

Jamie Brandon – Creative takes on programming language design and tooling.

Rachel Kroll – Entertaining stories from real-world operations and infrastructure.

Mark Callaghan – In-depth analysis of databases and storage engines.

Phil Eaton – Approachable posts on databases, storage, and distributed systems.

Evan Jones – Honest reflections on building reliable software systems.

Justin Jaffray – Concise, practical notes on distributed systems and infrastructure.

Abhinav Upadhyay – Practical notes and experiments in systems and programming.

Alex Miller – Thoughtful essays on databases, transactions, and distributed systems.

Tanel Poder – Deep technical dives into database performance and internals.

Varun Upadhyay – Deep technical dives into Distributed Computing concepts

Machine Learning #

Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, LLMs, ML for Systems, Systems for ML etc.

Chris Olah – Deep, visual explanations of neural networks and interpretability.

Andrej Karpathy – Insightful posts and videos on deep learning, AI, and practical ML engineering.

Lilian Weng – In-depth, approachable explorations of modern ML topics.

Sebastian Ruder – Excellent coverage of NLP, transfer learning, and research trends.

Sebastian Raschka – Practical guides and tutorials on machine learning and deep learning.

Nathan Lambert / Interconnects – Thoughtful commentary on AI research and industry trends.

Explosion AI – Blog from the creators of spaCy, covering NLP and ML engineering.

Jay Alammar – Visual, intuitive explanations of ML and NLP concepts.

Language Models Newsletter – Updates and insights on large language models.

Erik Bernhardsson – Practical and theoretical posts on ML, data, and engineering.

Eugene Yan – Applied ML, data science, and curated reading lists.

Jeremy Jordan – Clear guides on distributed training and ML engineering.

Denny Britz – Tutorials and experiments in deep learning and reinforcement learning.

Alex Smola – Research and commentary from a leading ML academic.

Radek Osmulski – Hands-on lessons and reflections on practical deep learning.

Daniel Bourke – Beginner-friendly guides and resources for learning ML.

Chip Huyen – Essays on ML systems, production, and real-world deployment.

ICLR Blogs – Research Paper Style blogs from ICLR’s Blogpost track.
